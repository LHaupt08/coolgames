An Introduction

I am Lukas. I've been into video games since the age of around 5, and have primarily used Nintendo consoles (ha, Nintendo baby) throughout my (somewhat short) life.

We had a Wii when I was born, but I did not really care about it at the time. However, I do have memories of watching my brother and dad playing Wii Play (the tanks minigame). I recall watching my brother beat the 20th level (a feat I have yet to surpass!) during those times.

Around my fifth or sixth birthday, one of my presents was Super Smash Bros Brawl. While it’s not exactly a popular game in the series, it was the game that got me into, well, gaming. The Wii is still a console I play on to this day, playing Skylanders, Fishing Resort, Speed Racer, the Wii version of Go Vacation, and the Wii-branded games.

About 6-7 years ago, I got a Wii U (which I love, even though I don’t have a lot of games for it), and around 2021, I bought a Switch. I don’t have a particular favorite or least favorite, as all of them have made me happy. Despite their crappy business practices, thanks, Nintendo.
